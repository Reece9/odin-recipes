Recipe for the perfect lasagne

How to make the perfect lasagne

Lasagne is a classic italian dish made up of pasta, ground beef, different sauces and tomatoes

For best results, cook this recipe 6 hours before serving!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  • 250g beef mince
  • 250g pork mince
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 200ml red wine
  • 9-12 lasagne sheets
  • 50g parmesan, grated
